Help & FAQs

These are sample questions and answers.

How do I sign in?

Once the real system is live, sign in using your JMU e-ID and password. For now, use some of the available test credentials.

How do I ask a question?

Go to the Question List and click the Ask Question button on that page. Make sure to give as much information as you can without being too verbose to allow TAs to identify how to best help you.

How do I add a TA?

Any faculty member may add one or more TAs by signing in and going into the system configuration page.
