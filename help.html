Help & FAQs

These are sample questions and answers.

How do I sign in?

Once the real system is live, sign in using your JMU e-ID and password. For now, use any credentials and choose your desired role.

How do I ask a question?

Sign in and then choose your professor and class, and provide information about the assignment and your particular question. Being detailed yet brief will your question will allow TAs to identify how to best help.

How do I add a TA?

Sign in as a faculty member and choose the "Add TA" button in the Configure System panel.
